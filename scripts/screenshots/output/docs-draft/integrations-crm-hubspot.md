# HubSpot Integration

Connect HubSpot CRM to sync companies, contacts, and deals. Configure sync settings and leverage HubSpot data for ABM campaigns.

This guide walks you through the hubspot integration with screenshots at every step.

---

## Step 1: Integrations Hub

Connect Abmatic AI with your other marketing and sales tools. Available integrations include CRMs (Salesforce, HubSpot), ad platforms, and more.

![Integrations Hub](/img/screenshots/verified/integrations-hub.png)

*Integrations Hub - This is at Integrations (app.abmatic.ai/integrations)*

### How to Get Here

Click "Integrations" in the left sidebar (under Settings area).

### What You Can Do

- View all available integrations
- Connect your CRM (Salesforce or HubSpot)
- Set up ad platform integrations (LinkedIn, Google)
- Configure Slack notifications
- Manage existing connections
- View sync status and logs

### Understanding the Data

Available integrations with connection status (connected/not connected) and configuration options.

### Next Step

Connect your CRM first - this enables bi-directional sync of accounts, contacts, and activity data.

:::tip Marketing Context
CRM integration is essential for ABM. Sync your target accounts, push visitor data to sales, and close the loop on attribution.
:::

---

## Step 2: HubSpot Integration

Configure your HubSpot integration. Connect Abmatic AI to HubSpot for syncing companies, contacts, and deal data.

![HubSpot Integration](/img/screenshots/verified/integration-hubspot.png)

*HubSpot Integration - This is at Integrations > HubSpot (app.abmatic.ai/integrations/hubspot)*

### How to Get Here

Go to Integrations and click on the HubSpot card, or navigate directly.

### What You Can Do

- Connect to HubSpot via OAuth
- Sync Companies and Contacts
- Map properties between systems
- Import from HubSpot lists
- Push visitor data to HubSpot timeline
- Sync custom properties
- View sync status and logs

### Understanding the Data

Connection status, property mappings, sync configuration, and history.

### Next Step

Connect via OAuth, configure property mappings, and import your target account list from HubSpot.

:::tip Marketing Context
HubSpot users: sync your companies and contacts for unified ABM data. Push website visitor intelligence directly into HubSpot timelines.
:::
