# Salesforce Integration

Connect Salesforce to sync accounts, contacts, and opportunities. Set up bi-directional sync, field mapping, and import your existing CRM data.

This guide walks you through the salesforce integration with screenshots at every step.

---

## Step 1: Integrations Hub

Connect Abmatic AI with your other marketing and sales tools. Available integrations include CRMs (Salesforce, HubSpot), ad platforms, and more.

![Integrations Hub](/img/screenshots/verified/integrations-hub.png)

*Integrations Hub - This is at Integrations (app.abmatic.ai/integrations)*

### How to Get Here

Click "Integrations" in the left sidebar (under Settings area).

### What You Can Do

- View all available integrations
- Connect your CRM (Salesforce or HubSpot)
- Set up ad platform integrations (LinkedIn, Google)
- Configure Slack notifications
- Manage existing connections
- View sync status and logs

### Understanding the Data

Available integrations with connection status (connected/not connected) and configuration options.

### Next Step

Connect your CRM first - this enables bi-directional sync of accounts, contacts, and activity data.

:::tip Marketing Context
CRM integration is essential for ABM. Sync your target accounts, push visitor data to sales, and close the loop on attribution.
:::

## Step 2: Salesforce Integration

Configure your Salesforce integration. Connect Abmatic AI to your Salesforce org for bi-directional data sync of accounts, contacts, and activities.

![Salesforce Integration](/img/screenshots/verified/integration-salesforce.png)

*Salesforce Integration - This is at Integrations > Salesforce (app.abmatic.ai/integrations/salesforce)*

### How to Get Here

Go to Integrations and click on the Salesforce card, or navigate directly.

### What You Can Do

- Connect to your Salesforce org via OAuth
- Configure which objects to sync (Accounts, Contacts, Leads)
- Map Abmatic fields to Salesforce fields
- Set sync direction (one-way or bi-directional)
- Configure sync frequency
- View sync logs and troubleshoot issues
- Import accounts/contacts from Salesforce campaigns

### Understanding the Data

Connection status, field mappings, sync settings, and recent sync history.

### Next Step

Connect using OAuth, then configure field mappings. Start with Accounts to sync your target account list.

:::tip Marketing Context
Essential for ABM. Sync target accounts from Salesforce, push visitor intelligence back to sales, and ensure data consistency.
:::
