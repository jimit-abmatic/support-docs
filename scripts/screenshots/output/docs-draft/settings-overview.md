# Settings Overview

Configure your Abmatic AI account

This guide walks you through the settings overview with screenshots at every step.

---

## Step 1: Account Settings

Manage your Abmatic AI account information including company details, domain settings, and preferences.

![Account Settings](/img/screenshots/verified/settings-account.png)

*Account Settings - This is at Settings > Account (app.abmatic.ai/settings/account)*

### How to Get Here

Click the gear icon or "Settings" in the sidebar, then select "Account".

### What You Can Do

- Update company name and website
- Manage account domain settings
- Configure data collection preferences
- View account ID and API credentials
- Update notification preferences

### Understanding the Data

Account information: company name, domain, account ID, and configuration options.

### Next Step

Ensure your company details are accurate for proper visitor matching.

:::tip Marketing Context
Keep your account settings current, especially the domain settings for accurate company identification.
:::

---

## Step 2: User Management

Manage team members who have access to your Abmatic AI account. Invite new users and manage permissions.

![User Management](/img/screenshots/verified/settings-users.png)

*User Management - This is at Settings > Users (app.abmatic.ai/settings/users)*

### How to Get Here

Click "Settings" then select "Users".

### What You Can Do

- View all users on your account
- Invite new team members
- Set user roles and permissions
- Remove users
- Resend invitations

### Understanding the Data

User list with: name, email, role, status (active/pending), and last login.

### Next Step

Invite your marketing team members and sales stakeholders who need access.

:::tip Marketing Context
Give your whole ABM team access. Consider inviting sales for Reveal access and executives for dashboard viewing.
:::

---

## Step 3: Usage & Billing

View your account usage metrics and billing information. Track API credits, visitor limits, and subscription details.

![Usage & Billing](/img/screenshots/verified/settings-usage.png)

*Usage & Billing - This is at Settings > Usage (app.abmatic.ai/settings/usage)*

### How to Get Here

Click "Settings" then select "Usage".

### What You Can Do

- View current usage vs. limits
- Check API credit consumption
- See billing history
- Upgrade plan if needed
- Download invoices

### Understanding the Data

Usage metrics: visitors tracked, API calls, reveals used, and remaining credits.

### Next Step

Monitor usage to ensure you don't exceed limits. Upgrade if approaching thresholds.

:::tip Marketing Context
Keep an eye on usage, especially visitor reveals and API credits, to plan for scaling.
:::

## Step 4: Custom Fields

Define custom fields for accounts and contacts to track data specific to your business needs.

![Custom Fields](/img/screenshots/verified/settings-custom-fields.png)

*Custom Fields - This is at Settings > Custom Fields (app.abmatic.ai/settings/custom-fields)*

### How to Get Here

Click "Settings" then select "Custom Fields".

### What You Can Do

- Create custom account fields
- Create custom contact fields
- Edit field names and types
- Map to CRM custom fields
- Delete unused fields

### Understanding the Data

List of custom fields for accounts and contacts with names and types.

### Next Step

Add custom fields that match your CRM structure for seamless data sync.

:::tip Marketing Context
Align your Abmatic data model with your CRM. Add fields like "Account Tier", "ICP Score", or "Target Persona".
:::

## Step 5: Notification Settings

Configure how and when you receive notifications about account activity, conversions, and system events.

![Notification Settings](/img/screenshots/verified/settings-notifications.png)

*Notification Settings - This is at Settings > Notifications (app.abmatic.ai/settings/notifications)*

### How to Get Here

Click "Settings" then select "Notifications".

### What You Can Do

- Enable/disable email notifications
- Configure Slack notifications
- Set notification frequency
- Choose which events trigger notifications
- Set up alerts for specific accounts

### Understanding the Data

Notification preferences and current settings.

### Next Step

Set up Slack notifications to get real-time alerts when target accounts visit.

:::tip Marketing Context
Get instant alerts when hot accounts visit. Perfect for sales follow-up at the right moment.
:::

## Step 6: Slack Integration Settings

Configure Slack integration for real-time notifications. Get alerts in Slack when target accounts visit or convert.

![Slack Integration Settings](/img/screenshots/verified/settings-slack.png)

*Slack Integration Settings - This is at Settings > Slack (app.abmatic.ai/settings/slack)*

### How to Get Here

Click "Settings" then select "Slack".

### What You Can Do

- Connect your Slack workspace
- Choose notification channels
- Configure which events trigger Slack messages
- Set up account-specific alerts
- Disconnect Slack

### Understanding the Data

Slack connection status, configured channels, and notification settings.

### Next Step

Connect Slack and set up a channel for target account visit notifications.

:::tip Marketing Context
Essential for real-time ABM. Sales can get instant Slack alerts when target accounts are on your site.
:::

## Step 7: Data Export

Export your Abmatic AI data for analysis or backup. Download accounts, contacts, and activity data.

![Data Export](/img/screenshots/verified/settings-export.png)

*Data Export - This is at Settings > Export (app.abmatic.ai/settings/export)*

### How to Get Here

Click "Settings" then select "Export".

### What You Can Do

- Export account data to CSV
- Export contact data
- Export activity and engagement data
- Schedule recurring exports
- Download previous exports

### Understanding the Data

Export options and history of previous exports.

### Next Step

Export data periodically for backup or for analysis in other tools.

:::tip Marketing Context
Use exports for advanced analysis in Excel or for data warehouse integration.
:::
