# Target Groups & Lists

Create, manage, and update account and contact lists. Learn about manual lists, filter-based dynamic lists, and auto-generated Associated Lists.

This guide walks you through the target groups & lists with screenshots at every step.

---

## Step 1: Target Groups

Target groups combine accounts and contacts for advanced segmentation. You can create groups with specific criteria for campaign targeting.

![Target Groups](/img/screenshots/verified/groups-list.png)

*Target Groups - This is at Audiences > Groups (app.abmatic.ai/groups)*

### How to Get Here

Click "Groups" in the left sidebar under the Audiences section.

### What You Can Do

- View all target groups
- Create new groups with combined account and contact criteria
- Edit group definitions
- Use groups for campaign targeting

### Understanding the Data

List of target groups with names, member counts, and creation dates.

### Next Step

Create groups to define complex targeting criteria that combine account and contact attributes.

:::tip Marketing Context
Groups are powerful for advanced ABM. Example: "VP+ at Enterprise Tech Companies" combines account size with contact seniority.
:::

---

## Step 2: All Accounts AG Grid View

A powerful data grid showing all accounts in your database. This uses AG Grid with advanced filtering, sorting, column customization, and charting capabilities.

![All Accounts AG Grid View](/img/screenshots/verified/accounts-all-grid.png)

*All Accounts AG Grid View - This is at Accounts > All (app.abmatic.ai/accounts/all)*

### How to Get Here

Navigate to Accounts and click "All Accounts" or go directly via URL.

### What You Can Do

- Use column filters to search by any attribute (click the filter icon on any column)
- Multi-select accounts using checkboxes to bulk add to lists
- Sort by clicking column headers
- Drag columns to rearrange layout
- Right-click for context menu options
- Create charts from selected data
- Export filtered data to CSV
- Save filter presets for quick access

### Understanding the Data

Account data in rows with columns for: Company Name, Website, Industry, Employee Count, Location, Engagement Score, Last Visit Date, Technologies, and custom fields.

### Next Step

Use filters to find accounts matching your ICP criteria. Select accounts and add them to a target list, or export for further analysis.

:::tip Marketing Context
This is your account intelligence powerhouse. Use filters like "Industry = SaaS AND Employees > 100 AND Visited Last 30 Days" to find your hottest target accounts.
:::

## Step 3: All Contacts AG Grid View

A powerful data grid showing all contacts in your database with professional information, engagement data, and filtering capabilities.

![All Contacts AG Grid View](/img/screenshots/verified/contacts-all-grid.png)

*All Contacts AG Grid View - This is at Contacts > All (app.abmatic.ai/contacts/all)*

### How to Get Here

Navigate to Contacts and click "All Contacts" or go directly via URL.

### What You Can Do

- Filter contacts by job title, seniority, company, department
- Multi-select contacts for bulk actions
- Sort by name, company, title, or engagement
- View LinkedIn profiles directly
- Add selected contacts to lists
- Export filtered data
- Create charts showing distribution by title/department

### Understanding the Data

Contact data with: Name, Email, Job Title, Company, Department, Seniority, LinkedIn URL, Last Activity Date, and engagement metrics.

### Next Step

Filter to find decision-makers at target accounts (e.g., "Title contains VP AND Company in Target List"). Add to contact lists for personalized campaigns.

:::tip Marketing Context
Find and track the buying committee at target accounts. Filter by seniority (VP+) and department (Marketing, IT) to focus on decision-makers.
:::

## Step 4: Create Account List Modal

The modal for creating new account lists. Choose how you want to build your list: from filters, CSV upload, CRM import, or AI recommendations.

![Create Account List Modal](/img/screenshots/verified/account-create-modal.png)

*Create Account List Modal - This modal appears over the Accounts page*

### How to Get Here

Click "Create Collection" or the "+" button on the Accounts page.

### What You Can Do

- Build from Filters - Create dynamic lists based on account criteria
- Upload CSV - Import accounts from a spreadsheet
- Import from CRM - Pull accounts from Salesforce or HubSpot
- AI Recommendations - Get AI-suggested accounts based on your ICP

### Understanding the Data

List creation options with descriptions.

### Next Step

Choose the method that fits your needs. CSV for existing lists, Filters for dynamic segments, CRM for synced lists.

:::tip Marketing Context
This is where ABM targeting starts. Most teams start with a CSV of target accounts, then graduate to CRM sync and AI recommendations.
:::

## Step 5: Create Contact List Modal

The modal for creating new contact lists. Similar to accounts, choose how to build your list.

![Create Contact List Modal](/img/screenshots/verified/contact-create-modal.png)

*Create Contact List Modal - This modal appears over the Contacts page*

### How to Get Here

Click "Create Collection" or the "+" button on the Contacts page.

### What You Can Do

- Build from Filters - Create lists based on contact criteria
- Upload CSV - Import contacts from a spreadsheet
- Import from CRM - Pull contacts from Salesforce or HubSpot

### Understanding the Data

List creation options with descriptions.

### Next Step

Choose your method. CSV for existing contact lists, CRM for synced contacts.

:::tip Marketing Context
Build persona-based lists (e.g., "CMOs at Target Accounts") for personalized campaigns.
:::
