# Company Identification

See which companies visit your website

This guide walks you through the company identification with screenshots at every step.

---

## Step 1: Company Reveal (Identified Accounts)

See which companies are visiting your website in real-time. Abmatic AI identifies anonymous visitors and shows you the companies behind the traffic.

![Company Reveal (Identified Accounts)](/img/screenshots/verified/reveal-accounts.png)

*Company Reveal (Identified Accounts) - This is at Reveal > Accounts (app.abmatic.ai/reveal/accounts)*

### How to Get Here

Click "Reveal" in the left sidebar, then select "Accounts" (or it defaults here).

### What You Can Do

- View companies visiting your website
- See visit details: pages viewed, time on site, visit frequency
- Filter by industry, size, location
- Add visiting companies to target lists
- View company profiles and intelligence
- Export visitor data

### Understanding the Data

Identified companies with: company name, industry, size, location, pages visited, visit time, and engagement score.

### Next Step

Review visiting companies daily to identify high-intent accounts. Add engaged visitors to target lists for personalization.

:::tip Marketing Context
This is ABM intelligence gold. See which target accounts are researching you, which pages they view, and how engaged they are - even before they fill out a form.
:::

---

## Step 2: Contact Reveal (Identified Contacts)

See individual contacts visiting your website. When enabled, Abmatic AI can identify specific people from target accounts.

![Contact Reveal (Identified Contacts)](/img/screenshots/verified/reveal-contacts.png)

*Contact Reveal (Identified Contacts) - This is at Reveal > Contacts (app.abmatic.ai/reveal/contacts)*

### How to Get Here

Click "Reveal" in the left sidebar, then select "Contacts".

### What You Can Do

- View identified contacts visiting your site
- See their job title, company, and engagement
- View their visit history and pages viewed
- Add to contact lists
- Sync with your CRM

### Understanding the Data

Identified contacts with: name, title, company, email (if available), LinkedIn, and visit details.

### Next Step

Review high-value visitors and pass hot leads to sales. Use this data for personalized outreach.

:::tip Marketing Context
Know exactly WHO from your target accounts is visiting. This enables sales to reach out at the perfect moment with relevant context.
:::
