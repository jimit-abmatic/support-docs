# AI Agents Overview

Use AI to power your ABM program

## Step 1: AI Agents Hub

Interact with Abmatic AI's intelligent agents. These AI assistants can help with account research, campaign recommendations, and more.

![AI Agents Hub](/img/screenshots/verified/ai-agents-hub.png)

*AI Agents Hub - This is at AI Agents (app.abmatic.ai/ai-agents)*

### How to Get Here

Click "AI Agents" in the left sidebar (at the top, with the sparkle icon).

### What You Can Do

- Chat with ABM Agent for account research and recommendations
- Get AI-powered campaign suggestions
- Research target accounts with AI assistance
- Generate personalization copy
- Ask questions about your data

### Understanding the Data

AI agent selection with available capabilities.

### Next Step

Start a conversation with the ABM Agent to get account recommendations or campaign ideas.

:::tip Marketing Context
Your AI-powered ABM assistant. Use it to scale your account research and get data-driven recommendations.
:::

## Step 2: AI Agents Settings

Configure the behavior of AI agents. Set context, preferences, and constraints for AI-powered features.

![AI Agents Settings](/img/screenshots/verified/settings-ai-agents.png)

*AI Agents Settings - This is at Settings > AI Agents (app.abmatic.ai/settings/ai-agents)*

### How to Get Here

Click "Settings" then select "AI Agents".

### What You Can Do

- Set AI context and company information
- Configure agent behavior
- Set guardrails and constraints
- Enable/disable specific AI features

### Understanding the Data

AI configuration options and current settings.

### Next Step

Add company context to help AI provide more relevant recommendations.

:::tip Marketing Context
Customize AI to understand your ICP, messaging, and ABM strategy for better recommendations.
:::
