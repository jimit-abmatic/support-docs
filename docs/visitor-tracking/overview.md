# Visitor Tracking Overview

**Turn anonymous website traffic into qualified pipeline.** Abmatic AI's visitor tracking identifies companies and contacts visiting your website in real-time — even when they never fill out a form. Stop guessing who's interested and start engaging prospects at the first sign of buying intent.

**The result:** Sales teams focus on accounts already showing interest, marketing personalizes experiences in real-time, and pipeline grows faster.

## How De-Anonymization Works (In Simple Terms)

When someone visits your website, they are anonymous to you — but not to Abmatic. Here is how we turn unknown visitors into actionable leads:

### Step 1: Company Identification (IP-Based)

Every device connected to the internet has an IP address. Companies use dedicated IP ranges that can be mapped back to them. When a visitor lands on your site, Abmatic matches their IP address against a database of 40M+ companies to identify the organization behind the visit.

**What this means for you:** Even if a visitor never fills out a form, you will know that "someone from OpenText visited your pricing page three times this week."

### Step 2: Contact Matching

Abmatic goes beyond company-level data to identify the specific people visiting your site using multiple methods:

| Method | How It Works | Example |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| **Form submissions** | Visitor fills out any form on your site | A demo request links their email, name, and company to all past and future visits |
| **Email link clicks** | Visitor clicks a link in your marketing email | The click-through URL identifies them, and Abmatic connects this to their browsing session |
| **Contact database matching** | IP identification combined with professional contact databases | Abmatic cross-references the identified company with known contacts who match the visitor profile |

### Step 3: Enrichment and Scoring

Once identified, Abmatic automatically enriches the record with firmographic data (industry, revenue, employee count) and calculates an engagement score based on visit frequency, pages viewed, and content depth.

### Understanding Confidence Levels

Not every identification is equally certain. Abmatic assigns a confidence level so you know how much to trust each match and can act accordingly:

| Confidence | What It Means | Your Best Move |
|------------|---------------|----------------|
| **Very High** (71-100%) | Direct match from a dedicated corporate IP range | Prioritize for immediate sales outreach |
| **High** (51-70%) | Strong signals with high certainty | Add to your target account lists and sequences |
| **Moderate** (31-50%) | Good match, some uncertainty | Include in personalization and ad campaigns |
| **Low** (21-30%) | Possible match, worth monitoring | Use for awareness campaigns; verify before direct outreach |
| **Very Low** (6-20%) | Weak signal | Monitor for repeat visits that increase confidence |

:::tip Focus Where It Counts
Start your outreach with Very High and High confidence matches for the best results. Use Moderate matches in advertising campaigns where broader reach is acceptable and false positives cost less.
:::

## Best Practices for Acting on Reveal Data

### Speed Matters

The value of reveal data decays fast. A company that visited your pricing page today is far more likely to respond to outreach than one that visited two weeks ago. Build a daily review habit:

| When | What to Do | Why |
|------|-----------|-----|
| **Every morning** | Check Very High and High confidence visitors from the past 24 hours | Catch active buyers while intent is fresh |
| **Before sales calls** | Look up the prospect's account in Reveal | Walk into calls with full context on what they researched |
| **Weekly** | Review engagement trends and build new target lists | Spot emerging opportunities before competitors |

### Quality Over Quantity

Do not blast every revealed contact with a cold email. Use the data to be relevant:

- **Reference their behavior:** "I noticed your team has been researching [topic] — here is a resource that might help."
- **Match the contact to the message:** A VP of Engineering and a Director of Marketing at the same company need different pitches.
- **Respect confidence levels:** Outreach to a Very High confidence match is informed; outreach to a Very Low match is a shot in the dark.

### Connect the Dots Across Channels

Reveal data is most powerful when it feeds your broader go-to-market strategy:

- Push high-engagement accounts to **LinkedIn Matched Audiences** for retargeting
- Sync contacts to **Salesforce or HubSpot** to enrich your CRM pipeline
- Use company data to **personalize your website** with industry-specific messaging
- Build **email sequences** triggered by specific page visits
