# Welcome to Abmatic AI Documentation

Abmatic AI is a powerful Account-Based Marketing (ABM) and demand generation platform that helps marketing teams identify, target, and convert high-value accounts.

## What You Can Do with Abmatic AI

- **Identify Target Accounts** - Discover and prioritize companies visiting your website using IP-based company identification
- **Create Personalized Campaigns** - Build multi-channel campaigns across website, email, LinkedIn Ads, and Google Ads
- **Leverage AI Agents** - Use specialized AI assistants for campaign strategy, content analysis, and audience discovery
- **Track Conversions** - Measure campaign performance with comprehensive conversion tracking
- **Integrate Your Tools** - Connect with Salesforce, HubSpot, LinkedIn Ads, Google Ads, and more
