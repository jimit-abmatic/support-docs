# Home Dashboard

Your Home page is the first thing you see when you log in to Abmatic AI. It gives you an at-a-glance view of your entire ABM operation -- accounts, contacts, campaigns, reports, and collections -- so you can orient yourself and jump straight into the work that matters most.

![Home Overview tab showing summary cards and recently viewed items](/img/screenshots/home-overview-tab.png)

## Two Tabs, Two Perspectives

The Home page is organized into two tabs:

| Tab | What It Shows | Best For |
|-----|---------------|----------|
| **Overview** | Summary counts and recently viewed items across all areas | Quick orientation, jumping to recent work, daily check-ins |
| **Dashboard** | Customizable analytics with charts and tables from your reports | Performance monitoring, trend spotting, executive summaries |

---

## Overview Tab

The Overview tab is your command center. It loads automatically when you navigate to Home and surfaces the most important numbers and recent activity.

### Summary Cards

Five cards span the top of the page, each showing a live count:

| Card | What It Counts | Clicking It Goes To |
|------|----------------|---------------------|
| **Accounts** | Total accounts in your database | All Accounts view |
| **Contacts** | Total contacts across all lists | All Contacts view |
| **Reports** | Number of saved reports | Analytics Reports |
| **Campaigns** | Total campaigns (active + inactive) | Campaigns list |
| **Collections** | Account and contact collections | Accounts Collections |

These numbers update every time you visit the Home page, so you always see the latest totals.

### Recently Viewed Sections

Below the summary cards, five panels show items you recently interacted with:

| Section | What It Shows | Details Displayed |
|---------|---------------|-------------------|
| **Recently Viewed Accounts** | Last 3 accounts you opened | Company name, employee count, link to website |
| **Recently Viewed Contacts** | Last 3 contacts you viewed | Name, email, job title, LinkedIn link |
| **Recently Viewed Reports** | Last 3 reports you checked | Report name, report type |
| **Recently Viewed Campaigns** | Last 3 campaigns you opened | Campaign name, type, Active/Inactive status |
| **Recently Viewed Target Groups** | Last 3 collections you visited | Collection name, record count, source type, status |

Each item is clickable -- click any account, contact, report, campaign, or collection name to jump directly to its detail page.

:::tip Use Overview as Your Daily Starting Point
Open Abmatic AI, glance at your summary cards to spot any major changes, then click into whichever recently viewed item needs your attention. No need to navigate through menus for the things you work on most.
:::

## Dashboard Tab

The Dashboard tab turns your Home page into a customizable analytics hub. Pin your most important reports here as charts or tables so you can monitor performance without leaving the Home page.

![Home Dashboard tab with analytics charts and reports](/img/screenshots/home-dashboard-alt.png)

### What the Dashboard Includes

- **Report widgets** displayed as charts or tables, arranged in a drag-and-drop grid
- **Date range selector** to control the time period across all widgets at once (Last 7 Days, Last 30 Days, Last 60 Days, Custom Range)
- **Edit Dashboard** button to add, remove, or rearrange widgets
- **Refresh** button to pull the latest data into all widgets
- **Last updated** timestamp so you know how fresh the data is

### Adding Reports to Your Dashboard

1. Click the **Dashboard** tab on the Home page
2. Click **Edit Dashboard**
3. Click **+ Report** to open the Add Report dialog
4. Select a report from the dropdown (only reports with chart-enabled data appear)
5. Choose the display type: **Chart** (visual graph) or **Table** (data grid)
6. Click **Add Report** to place the widget on your dashboard
7. Drag the widget to reposition it, or resize it by dragging the edges
8. Click **Save Dashboard** when you are done

### Customizing Your Layout

While in edit mode:

| Action | How To |
|--------|--------|
| **Move a widget** | Grab the drag handle (six-dot icon) and drag it to a new position |
| **Remove a widget** | Click the X icon in the widget header |
| **Resize a widget** | Drag the bottom-right corner of any widget |
| **Open the source report** | Click the report name link with the arrow icon in the widget header |

You can add up to **20 report widgets** per dashboard.

### Filtering by Date Range

The date range selector in the top-right corner applies to all widgets simultaneously:

| Option | Time Period |
|--------|-------------|
| **Last 7 Days** | Past week |
| **Last 30 Days** | Past month |
| **Last 60 Days** | Past two months |
| **Last 90 Days** | Past quarter |
| **Custom** | Pick specific start and end dates from a calendar |

Changing the date range refreshes every widget on the dashboard with the updated time period.

:::info Paid Plan Feature
The Dashboard tab is available on paid plans. If you are on a free plan, you will see a prompt to upgrade. The Overview tab is available to all users.
:::

## Quick Actions from Home

The Home page connects you to every major area of Abmatic. Here are the fastest paths from Home:

| What You Want to Do | Path from Home |
|---------------------|----------------|
| Check a specific account | Click it in **Recently Viewed Accounts** |
| Review campaign performance | Click a campaign in **Recently Viewed Campaigns**, or switch to **Dashboard** tab |
| Build a new account list | Click the **Accounts** summary card, then **+ Account List** |
| View a report | Click it in **Recently Viewed Reports**, or add it to your **Dashboard** |
| Check your collections | Click the **Collections** summary card |
| See contact details | Click a contact in **Recently Viewed Contacts** |

## Getting Started from Home

If you are new to Abmatic AI, here is how to make the most of your Home page:

### First Week: Explore the Overview

1. **Check your summary cards** -- see how many accounts Abmatic has already identified from your website traffic
2. **Browse recently viewed items** -- as you explore accounts, campaigns, and reports, they will start appearing here for quick access
3. **Click through to details** -- use the Overview as a launchpad to learn where everything lives

### After Setup: Build Your Dashboard

Once you have campaigns running and reports created:

1. **Switch to the Dashboard tab** and click **Edit Dashboard**
2. **Add 3-5 key reports** that matter most to your team (e.g., Campaign Performance, Opportunities by Stage, Conversion by Campaign Type)
3. **Arrange them by priority** -- put the most critical metrics in the top-left where your eyes go first
4. **Set the date range** to match your reporting cadence (weekly, monthly, or quarterly)
5. **Save and share** -- your dashboard is personal to your account, so customize it for your workflow

:::tip Dashboard Ideas for Marketers
- **Pipeline dashboard**: Opportunities by Stage + Opportunities by Close Quarter + Campaign Performance
- **ABM dashboard**: Account Engagement by Tier + Campaign Conversions + Website Visitor Trends
- **Executive dashboard**: Revenue by Quarter + Campaign ROI + New Accounts by Source
:::
