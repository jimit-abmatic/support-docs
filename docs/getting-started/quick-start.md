# Quick Start Guide

Get started with Abmatic AI in just a few minutes. This guide walks you through the essential steps to set up your account and launch your first campaign.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:
- An Abmatic AI account ([sign up here](https://app.abmatic.ai/signup))
- Access to your website's HTML or tag manager (Google Tag Manager, etc.)
- (Optional) CRM credentials for Salesforce or HubSpot integration

## Step 1: Log In to Your Account

1. Go to [app.abmatic.ai](https://app.abmatic.ai)
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Sign In**

![Dashboard Overview](/img/screenshots/dashboard-overview.png)

## Step 2: Install the Tracking Script

The Abmatic tracking script identifies companies visiting your website and enables personalization.

1. Navigate to **Settings** → **Script Installation**
2. Copy your unique tracking script
3. Add the script to your website's `<head>` section or via Google Tag Manager

See [Install Tracking Script](/getting-started/install-script) for detailed instructions.

## Step 3: Connect Your CRM (Optional but Recommended)

Connecting your CRM enables two-way data sync and enriched targeting:

1. Go to **Integrations**
2. Select your CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, or ActiveCampaign)
3. Click **Connect** and authorize access
4. Configure field mappings

## Step 4: Create Your First Audience

Build a target account list to focus your marketing efforts:

1. Navigate to **Audiences** → **Accounts**
2. Click **Create Account List**
3. Choose your creation method:
   - **From Filters** - Use company attributes (size, industry, technology)
   - **From CRM** - Import from your connected CRM
   - **CSV Upload** - Upload a list of companies
4. Name your audience and save

## Step 5: Launch Your First Campaign

Create a personalized experience for your target accounts:

1. Go to **Campaigns**
2. Click **Create Campaign**
3. Select your campaign type:
   - **Website Personalization** - Dynamic content on your site
   - **Email Campaign** - Personalized email sequences
   - **LinkedIn Ads** - Targeted LinkedIn advertising
4. Select your target audience
5. Configure your campaign content
6. Click **Activate**
