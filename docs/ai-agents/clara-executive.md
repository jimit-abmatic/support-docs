# Clara - Chief Executive Agent

Clara is your strategic ABM advisor, providing high-level guidance and coordinating across all marketing functions.

## Capabilities

- **Strategic Planning**: Multi-turn conversations about ABM strategy
- **Cross-functional Insights**: Recommendations spanning campaigns, audiences, and performance
- **Executive Reporting**: High-level summaries and business impact analysis

## Example Conversations

### Strategic Planning
> **You**: "What should be my ABM priorities for Q2?"
>
> **Clara**: Based on your current pipeline and engagement data...

### Performance Review
> **You**: "How are our enterprise campaigns performing?"
>
> **Clara**: Looking at your enterprise segment campaigns...

## Best Practices

- Provide context about your business goals
- Ask follow-up questions to refine recommendations
- Use Clara for high-level strategy, specific agents for execution
