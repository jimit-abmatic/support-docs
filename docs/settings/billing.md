## Why Monitor Usage?

Understanding your usage helps you:
- **Optimize ROI** - See exactly where your credits are being used
- **Plan ahead** - Anticipate when you'll need additional capacity
- **Avoid interruptions** - Stay ahead of limits before they impact campaigns
- **Budget effectively** - Track consumption against your subscription

## Accessing Usage & Billing

Navigate to **Settings > Usage** from the sidebar to view your complete usage dashboard.

![Usage and Billing Dashboard](/img/screenshots/settings-usage.png)

## Credits Overview

The Credits section displays your consumption across all Abmatic AI services:

| Credit Type | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Apollo Contacts** | Credits used for contact enrichment via Apollo integration |
| **Campaigns** | Credits consumed by active personalization campaigns |
| **Open AI** | Credits consumed by AI-powered features (Clara, Alex, content generation) |
| **Abmatic Accounts** | Credits for company data enrichment and lookups |
| **Abmatic Contacts** | Credits for contact data enrichment services |
| **Abmatic IP Reveal** | Credits used for identifying anonymous website visitors |

Each credit type shows a clear **Used/Total Credits Used** format so you can instantly see your consumption status.

## Understanding Your Credits

### Apollo Contacts
Used when enriching contacts with data from the Apollo integration. Each contact lookup consumes one credit.

### Campaigns
Consumed by active personalization campaigns running on your website. Higher traffic and more complex campaigns use more credits.

### Open AI
Powers AI features throughout Abmatic including:
- Alex (AI SDR) research and conversations
- Clara (AI ABM) campaign recommendations
- AI-generated content for personalization

### Abmatic Accounts
Used for company identification and enrichment:
- Enriching account data with firmographic information
- Company lookups from various data sources

### Abmatic Contacts
Consumed when enriching contact records with professional data:
- Job title verification
- Contact information enrichment
- Professional profile data

### Abmatic IP Reveal
The core visitor identification feature:
- Identifying companies visiting your website
- Converting anonymous traffic into known accounts
- Real-time visitor intelligence

## Managing Your Subscription

### Need More Credits?

If you're approaching your limits, contact your account manager or reach out to sales@abmatic.ai to discuss:
- Higher credit allocations
- Increased plan limits
- Enterprise packages with custom allocations

### Billing Questions

For invoice requests, payment method updates, or billing inquiries:
- **Email**: billing@abmatic.ai
- **Support**: Contact your customer success manager

## Best Practices

### For Marketing Teams
- Review usage weekly to ensure campaigns aren't running low on credits
- Monitor Open AI credits if using AI agents heavily
- Coordinate major campaign launches with your credit availability

### For Operations Teams
- Set calendar reminders to check usage regularly
- Document credit consumption patterns for budgeting
- Identify which services consume the most credits and optimize accordingly

### For Sales Teams
- Keep an eye on Apollo Contacts if doing heavy prospecting
- Monitor Abmatic Contacts usage for lead enrichment activities
