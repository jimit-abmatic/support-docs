# Audience Insights

Turn anonymous website traffic into actionable intelligence. Abmatic's Audience Insights gives you a complete view of which companies are visiting your site, what content resonates with them, and when they're ready to buy—so you can prioritize the right accounts and accelerate your pipeline.

## Why Audience Insights Matters

Understanding your audience is the foundation of effective ABM. With Audience Insights, you can:

- **Identify high-intent accounts** before they fill out a form
- **Prioritize sales outreach** based on actual engagement data
- **Refine your ICP** using real visitor behavior, not assumptions
- **Prove marketing ROI** with clear account-level attribution
- **Align sales and marketing** around the same data

## Accessing Audience Insights

Navigate to **Analytics > Reports** to access your audience data. Create custom reports to analyze visitor behavior, engagement trends, and account activity.

![Analytics Reports - View and create reports for audience insights](/img/screenshots/analytics-reports.png)

## Account Activity Overview

See exactly which target accounts are engaging with your website and how deeply they're researching your solution.

### Top Engaged Accounts

Instantly identify your hottest prospects by their engagement level:

| Metric | What It Tells You |
|--------|-------------------|
| **Page Views** | Total content consumption—higher counts indicate deeper research |
| **Sessions** | Visit frequency—multiple sessions signal ongoing evaluation |
| **Time on Site** | Engagement depth—longer visits mean genuine interest |
| **Pages per Session** | Research thoroughness—prospects exploring multiple solutions look at more pages |

### Activity Trends

Spot patterns and opportunities in your audience behavior:

- **Daily/weekly/monthly views** — Understand traffic patterns and campaign impact
- **Period comparison** — Measure growth and identify what's working
- **Seasonal patterns** — Plan campaigns around when your audience is most active

## Visitor Composition

Understand who's visiting your site at a glance. This data helps you validate your ICP assumptions and discover new market opportunities.

### By Industry

See which industries show the most interest in your solution. Use this to:
- Validate your target market assumptions
- Discover untapped industry verticals
- Create industry-specific campaigns and content

### By Company Size

Understand whether you're attracting the right-sized companies:

| Segment | Employee Count | What It Means |
|---------|---------------|---------------|
| **Enterprise** | 1,000+ | Large deal potential, longer sales cycles |
| **Mid-Market** | 200-999 | Balanced opportunity and sales velocity |
| **SMB** | 50-199 | Faster decisions, volume play |
| **Small Business** | <50 | High volume, self-serve potential |

### By Location

Geographic insights help you:
- Plan territory assignments for sales
- Identify expansion opportunities
- Optimize campaign timing for time zones

## Engagement Analysis

### High-Intent Signals

Stop guessing which accounts are ready to buy. These behavioral signals indicate genuine purchase intent:

| Signal | What It Means | Recommended Action |
|--------|--------------|-------------------|
| **Pricing page visits** | Evaluating budget fit | Sales should reach out with pricing discussion |
| **Multiple sessions** | Active evaluation | Add to nurture campaigns |
| **Demo page visits** | Ready to see the product | Prioritize for immediate outreach |
| **High page views** | Deep research | Likely comparing solutions—share differentiation content |
| **Return visits** | Still considering | Time-sensitive—competitors may be in play |

### Content Consumption

Discover what content drives engagement with your best accounts:

- **Most viewed pages** — Double down on topics that resonate
- **Blog posts driving engagement** — Create more content on winning themes
- **Resources downloaded** — Understand what information prospects need
- **Case studies viewed** — Know which proof points matter to your audience

## Funnel Analysis

### Visitor Journey

Visualize how accounts progress through your funnel—and identify where they get stuck:

| Stage | Behavior Signals | Opportunity |
|-------|-----------------|-------------|
| **Awareness** | First visit | Capture attention with personalized content |
| **Interest** | Multiple pages, return visits | Nurture with targeted campaigns |
| **Consideration** | Pricing, demo pages | Sales-ready—engage immediately |
| **Intent** | Form submission, contact | Close the deal |

### Conversion Paths

Understand exactly how your best customers found and evaluated you:

- **Common page sequences** — Replicate successful journeys for other accounts
- **Sessions to conversion** — Set realistic sales timeline expectations
- **Time to conversion** — Benchmark your sales velocity

## Segment Comparison

### Compare Audiences

Answer critical questions about your audience with side-by-side comparisons:

| Comparison | What You'll Learn |
|-----------|------------------|
| Target accounts vs. all visitors | Are your target accounts more engaged than average? |
| Enterprise vs. SMB | Which segment has better conversion rates? |
| Industry A vs. Industry B | Where should you focus expansion? |

### Performance Benchmarks

Track key metrics across segments to optimize your strategy:

- **Engagement rates** — Which audiences respond best to your content?
- **Conversion rates** — Where are you winning and losing deals?
- **Time to conversion** — Which segments move fastest through your funnel?

## Exporting Data

### Available Exports

Take your audience data wherever you need it:

| Export Type | Best For |
|------------|----------|
| **Account activity (CSV)** | CRM enrichment, sales prospecting lists |
| **Engagement summary (PDF)** | Executive reporting, stakeholder updates |
| **Raw visit data** | Custom analysis, data warehouse integration |

### Scheduling Reports

Automate your reporting workflow:

1. Navigate to **Analytics > Reports**
2. Click **Create Report**
3. Select the audience metrics you need
4. Set your schedule (weekly for active campaigns, monthly for executive reviews)
5. Choose delivery: email for stakeholders, Slack for real-time team updates

## Putting Insights Into Action

### For Marketing Teams

Transform insights into better targeting:

- **Refine your ICP** — Let actual engagement data (not assumptions) define your ideal customer
- **Discover new verticals** — Find industries you hadn't considered that are already interested
- **Find lookalike accounts** — Use your best engaged accounts to identify similar prospects

### For Content Strategy

Create content your audience actually wants:

- **Double down on winners** — Produce more content on topics that drive engagement
- **Fill the gaps** — Identify questions your audience has that you haven't answered
- **Personalize at scale** — Tailor content based on segment preferences

### For Sales Teams

Turn engagement data into closed deals:

- **Prioritize intelligently** — Focus on accounts showing buying behavior
- **Personalize outreach** — Reference specific content they've consumed
- **Time it right** — Reach out when accounts are actively researching
